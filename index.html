About Bishnoism

Bishnoism is a spiritual and environmental philosophy founded by Guru Jambheshwar Ji. It promotes harmony between humans, animals, and nature.

29 Principles

Protect Trees
Save Animals
Live Pure Life
Avoid Violence

History

The Bishnoi community is known for the sacrifice of 363 martyrs at Khejarli, who gave their lives to protect trees.
